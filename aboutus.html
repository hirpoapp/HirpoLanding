Why Hirpo

Hirpo is an AI-powered HR technology platform built to help companies manage people smarter — across recruitment, performance, learning, and analytics.

We believe HR should be simple, data-driven, and human-centered. That is why we blend intuitive design with Agentic AI capabilities so HR teams can automate operations, gain real-time insights, and make informed decisions that drive growth.

Built for growing teams

Designed for SMEs and scaling organizations, Hirpo delivers affordable tools that modernize HR operations without complexity.

Core modules

  • Employee Journey Dashboard – real-time visibility into people metrics and movements.
  • Performance & Satisfaction – track goals, competencies, and engagement.
  • Recruitment (ATS) – automate hiring and find the right candidates faster.
  • Learning (LMS) – manage onboarding, courses, and development plans.
  • HR Analytics – actionable insights and predictive dashboards for leadership.
