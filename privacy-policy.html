Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

At Hirpo, your privacy is our priority. We respect the confidentiality of every user of our platform and services, and we clearly explain how your data is collected, used, and protected. We may collect personal information such as your name, email, phone number, and company details, as well as technical information like IP address, browser type, and device data. This information is used solely to improve service quality, ensure security, and provide technical support. Your data is shared only with trusted technical partners or when required by law. Hirpo never sells or trades your personal information. You may contact us anytime to request deletion or correction of your data.
